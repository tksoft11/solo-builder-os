- **REDUCE:** 
  - Broad category tracking. 
- **RAISE:** 
  - Actionability. (e.g., "Here is the exact keyword to target, and here is the Amazon product to link to.")
  - Niche specificity.
- **CREATE:**

## 3. The Solo Moat (Unfair Advantage)

**The "Opinionated Curator" Moat.**
Software companies (like Exploding Topics) must remain objective and broad to serve a massive audience. As a solo builder, your moat is **Extreme Niche Authority and Subjectivity**. You don't just dump data; you filter it through the lens of a specific niche (e.g., "I analyzed 500 coffee tools, here are the only 3 worth promoting this week"). Data is a commodity; *Curation and Taste* are impossible for a scraper to copy.
