## 2. Blue Ocean Strategy Canvas

Instead of competing on "having more calendar features" (which Google Calendar wins), we eliminate, reduce, raise, and create new factors:

- **ELIMINATE:** 
  - Login screens and account creation.
  - Multi-year historical data.
  - Ability to add custom daily events (meetings, birthdays).
- **REDUCE:** 
  - Text density. Drop all legal jargon regarding holiday announcements.
- **RAISE:** 
  - Visual hierarchy of the *Very Next Holiday*.
  - Mobile-first load speed (Target < 1.0s LCP).
- **CREATE:** 
  - **The Leave Maximizer:** An automated suggestion engine that tells you exactly which dates to take annual leave to bridge gaps into 4-day or 5-day weekends.

## 3. The Solo Moat (Unfair Advantage)

As a solo developer, you cannot out-engineer Google, and you cannot out-SEO the Bank of Thailand. Your moat must be structural to your size:
**The "Micro-Tool" Velocity Moat.**
Because you have zero corporate overhead, no legal department, and no legacy code, you can build a Single Page Application (SPA) that does exactly one thing perfectly. Your moat is **Extreme Focus + Zero Friction**. When users realize they can get the answer in 0.8 seconds without accepting cookies or logging in, they will bookmark it over the heavy alternatives.

## 4. Positioning Statement

**For** Thai professionals who are burned out and desperately need a break, **Thai Holiday Hacker** is a minimalist web app **that** not only counts down the exact seconds to your next public holiday, **but also** calculates the exact days you should take annual leave to hack the calendar for 4-day weekends. **Unlike** official government websites or Google Calendar, **our product** provides instant psychological relief and actionable vacation strategy with zero clicks.
